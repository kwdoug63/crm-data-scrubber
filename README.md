# CRM Data Scrubber 🧼

This tool automates the tedious process of cleaning customer data. It ensures that your CRM stays organized and your marketing emails actually reach the right person.

## 🛠️ Key Functions
- **Name Normalization:** Automatically fixes "kEnNeTh" to "Kenneth".
- **Email Validation:** Identifies and flags improperly formatted email addresses.
- **Phone Formatting:** Standardizes numbers to a consistent (XXX) XXX-XXXX format.
- **Duplicate Detection:** Highlights potential repeat entries to save on CRM costs.

## 🚀 Why Businesses Need This
Messy data leads to bounced emails and unprofessional communication. This script ensures that every lead you import into **AI AutoPilot** or your CRM of choice is 100% professional.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python** (Core Logic)
- **Pandas** (High-performance data manipulation)
- **Regular Expressions (Re)** (Pattern matching for emails/phones)
